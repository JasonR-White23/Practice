About Solar Energy:

What is solar energy?

Solar energy is a renewable power source that is created by using the sun's light and heat and converting it into electricity.

To turn solar energy into electricity you must use one of the two most common type of technology when it comes to solar power. You may either use photovoltaics (PV) or concentrating solar-thermal power (CSP).

About Solar Panels:

How do solar panels work?

The sun emits solar radiation, solar panels are designed to encapsulate this electromagnetic radiation. When the materials that the solar panels are made of is exposed to the suns light it releases the electrons while also producing an electical charge which is then transfered to your home ready for use.
