A Tribute Page for Frodo Baggins

A photo of Frodo Baggins holding the one ring while standing in a forest.
  • 2968 TA (Third Age) - September 22: Frodo, the son of Drogo Baggins and Primula Brandybuck is born in The Shire
  • 2980 TA - Frodo's parents drown in a boating accident
  • 3001 TA - Frodo became Master of Bag End as well as the Ring Bearer
  • 3018 TA - Spetember 23: Frodo left Bag End and began his adventure as the Ring Bearer
  • 3019 TA - Mach 25: Frodo and Samwise arrive at Mount Doom, successfully destoying the One Ring
  • 3019 TA - October 30: Frodo and his company return to The Shire
  • 3020 TA - May 1: Frodo attends the wedding of Samwise and Rosie
  • 3021 TA - September 22: Frodo boards the White Ship with Bilbo, Gandalf, Elrond, and Galadriel and was allowed passage acrossthe sea to the Undying Lands
  • Fourth Age - Frodo spends the remainder of his life on Tol Eressëa
